Research

Concept validation through survey testing

A survey tested user needs and willingness to pay using a low fidelity mockup to ground feedback in a realistic experience.

Solution

Focused recipe output generation system

Users enter ingredients and basic preferences, and the system generates a small set of relevant recipes in real time. A character guides the cooking process step by step.

Clearer discovery of saved recipes

A high contrast card highlights where users can find saved recipes. Hick’s Law was used to reduce visual competition so the saved section stands out clearly. The character and message reinforce the feature without disrupting the layout.

Progressive disclosure for recipe details

The recipe page separates ingredients and instructions into a clear step by step flow. Information is revealed in a structured way so users can focus on cooking without being overwhelmed by everything at once.

Reduced perceived wait during generation

A character-led loading state shows Chef Yori animating alongside a clear generation indicator while the recipe is being created. This keeps users engaged during processing and reduces the feeling of waiting.
